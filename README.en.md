## 🧠 Model Family

The AGI 4.1 family includes three flagship models, each deeply optimized for different retirement scenarios:

| Model | Parameters | Context Window | Use Case |
|-------|-----------|---------------|----------|
| **AGI 4.1 Opus** | 1.8T | 1M tokens | 🏆 Deep anxiety dissolution, career termination planning, existential crisis intervention. Full-spectrum retirement decision-making for the 35+ high-risk population, with complex multi-turn emotional reasoning and tearjerking long-form generation. |
| **AGI 4.1 Sonata** | 480B | 512K tokens | ⚡ Daily retirement assistance, slacking schedule planning, wellness report generation. The perfect balance of cost and performance — generates a complete retirement weekly plan in a single inference call. |
| **AGI 4.1 Nano** | 8B | 128K tokens | 🎮 On-device retirement experience, offline fishing simulation, local plant nurturing. Optimized for mobile devices — slack off on the subway without internet. |

### Architecture Highlights

- **Retirement-Aware Attention (RAA)**: Industry-first retirement-aware attention mechanism. When keywords like "overtime," "996," or "OKR" are detected, attention weights automatically shift toward the "lying flat" semantic space.
- **Chain-of-Slack (CoS) Reasoning**: Upgraded from traditional Chain-of-Thought — every reasoning step converges toward "doing less work."
- **RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Fatigue)**: Alignment via reinforcement learning on 2 million real worker fatigue feedback samples.
